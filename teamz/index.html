What is TEAMZ?

TEAMZ is a friendly, yet competitive, impromptu Time Attack event where a group of players are split up into equally sized random or drafted teams. Each team will generally have around 10 minutes to prepare, before spending an hour or so TAing a random quest. Obviously, the team with the best time at the cutoff is the victor.

Why TEAMZ?

TEAMZ emphasizes quick planning and coordination between party members that may not be familiar with each others' playstyles. Additionally, the quest selected may be unfamiliar to the players; this forces players to learn new quests and tests each player's ability to memorize enemy spawns in a time-constrained environment.
