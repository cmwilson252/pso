Quest Records

This is a dedicated catalog of records from Ultma, 2014, Vanilla, and GC PSO. All records posted to this page have been checked for legitimacy.

Where/How to Get a Record?

These records are collected from many different places like "vanilla" server forums such as Ephinea or custom server forums like Ultima. 2014 times are collected solely from youtube. All times on this site have an accompanying video to satisfy the burden of proof. To get your own record you or your team must upload the run to youtube and post it to the site in which the run was done and it will be found. The top five times for each quest/stage will be kept.
