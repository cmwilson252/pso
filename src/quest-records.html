PSV: TA Teamz Player Cards

Normal Mode Rules

  • The in-game timer is used for timing each quest. Time starts when the in-game timer is activated and ends at the last meaningful input. This means the published time is when either the final monster is defeated, a text message is displayed signifying the conclusion of the quest, or manually deactivating the timer at a terminal. This is commonly known as Time Attack (TA).
  • Any exploits, such as, defeating monsters before the timer starts are not allowed.
  • Once the quest has started the whole party must complete the quest together. No one is to leave the game room before Dark Falz to manipulate Darvant waves. No one is to join the game while the quest is in progress.
  • In the 4-Player PB Combo category the players are to start with a 4-Player PB combo for Lv 81 Shifta/Deband before the quest has started. If the quest lasts longer than the S/D from the PB combo then the players may do another PB combo on quest progress, but not required if they choose not to do so. Note that even though it requires 4 players to perform the PB combo before the quest any number of players can start the quest together if a lower number of players is more beneficial, such as in TTF.
  • In the 2-Player category the highest level of S/D allowed on the characters is Lv 41 from a 2-Player PB combo. Using a PB combo is not required if the players choose not to do so. Additionally, the 2 players must be in the first and second character game slots. This is to prevent any manipulation on where each player is positioned at the start of each area.
  • In the two 1-Player categories the highest level of S/D allowed on the character is Lv 21 from a 1-Player PB as a Hunter or Ranger character, or Lv 30 as a Force character. Using a PB is not required if the player chooses not to do so. Additionally, the player must be in the first character game slot. This is to prevent any manipulation on where the player is positioned at the start of each area.

Challenge Mode Rules
  • In the 4-Player category reach the end of the stage. There are no additional restrictions.
  • In the 2-Player category since the team is limited to 2 players during the entire course of the game a third or fourth player cannot start with the original 2 players and drop items. Additionally, the 2 players must be in the first and second character game slots. This is to prevent skipping rooms in stages where the order of the characters is a factor, such as, 2C4.
