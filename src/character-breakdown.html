HUcast

Gear

Dark Flow MDH/NABH

  • Highest ATP sword.
  • Special attack is the highest single-target damage.
  • Combo unlocked.
  • Using S behind enemies as the spawn often kills them before they are able to move such as Gibbles.
  • Liching: using DF waves from a distance.

Trap Shooting Weapon: Charge Suppressed Gun/Charge Raygun MDH/NABH

  • Many other weapons with the handgun animation can substitute, but charge weapons help clean rooms up quickly without sacrificing HP or TP.

Vjaya MDH/NABH

  • Extremely high damage on groups. Optimal in many places with groups and sometimes even single targets if they die in 2 or less hits.
  • Combo is faster than DF.

Excalibur MDH/NABH

  • Extremely fast combo.
  • Maintain forward movement in places where only one monster or small group of three or less needs to die to progress.
  • High damage with Shifta/Zalure and even without.

Diska of Bravemen MDH/NABH

  • Good for lowering health for DF.
  • High AOE damage.

Berserk Type ME Mechguns/M&A 60 Vise MDH/NABH

  • Good for lowering HP for DF.
  • High DPS with Shifta/Zalure.

Psycho Ravens MDH/NABH NABD/ABMD

  • Consistent high DPS.

Lavis Blades

  • Fastest option to lower health and you can control each increment of the combos unlike Girasole/Inferno Girasole.

Twin Blaze

  • Allows early Gi Gue drops.
  • Stop enemy charges mostly when they first spawn like Delbiters.
  • Detonates traps.

S-Rank Zalure Gun

  • For bosses and high HP enemies like Epsilon, Girtablulu, etc if there is no FO around for 21 Zalure.

Morolian Blaster/Arrest Raygun/Arrest J-Cutter/Boomerang

  • Various forms of crowd control for groups and single targets.
  • SNS glitch with Boomerang and Arrest J-Cutter for higher proc rates.

Charge Type ME Mechgun

  • High DPS with Shifta/Zalure and particularly high with 4-way PB.

Tsumikiri J-Sword NABD/NMD

  • Special does not miss.
  • Good for clearing grouped monsters.

Hell Raygun/Asteron Striker/ Hell J-Cutter/Ultima Reaper/Kiss of Death

  • Various hell weapons for single targets and groups.
  • SNS glitch for Hell J-Cutter and Kiss of Death for higher proc rates.
  • Ultima Reaper for large groups. 15 targets.
  • Be wary of auto-aim on Asteron Striker as it can shoot others traps and aim to things that are sub-optimal.
  • Asteron Striker is one of the only options for rifle ranged weaponry for HUcast.

Slicer of Vengeance MDH/NABH

  • Higher damage than DoB in lined up groups, but does not lower health.

S-Reds

  • Gives Shift/Deband in solo runs.

Crush Cannon

  • Medium damage shot that covers large areas. Extremely effective with PB.
  • Degenerate.

Drill Launcher MH

  • One of the fastest ways for solo HUcast to lock Vol Opt 1 screens.

HUcast is the absolute highest DPS class(generally) in the game especially with +100 ATP and +10 ATA in addition from vanilla BB.

Combo unlocked Dark Flow and Charge Vulcans/Raygun outclass just about anything else other classes have to offer in most parties especially in PB situations.

Centurion/Battle giving a 110% speed boost compared to 2014s 50% or vanillas 40% furthers the dominance of this class.

Currently the pinnacle of the TA meta. HUct excels in 4P runs with proper planning.
